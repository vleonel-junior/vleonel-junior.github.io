Hello there! 👋

Welcome to my corner of the internet. If you’ve landed here, you’re likely someone who doesn’t just want to know *that* a model works, but *why* it works. 

In this blog, I will be sharing my journey through the fascinating landscapes of **Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Mathematical Engineering.** But this won't be your typical "how-to" guide. Here is what you can expect:

*   **Mathematical Foundations:** We will dive deep into formalization. Whether it's stochastic processes or complex optimization, we’ll face the math head-on.
*   **Intuition First:** Math can be complex, but the underlying intuition is what truly lasts. My goal is to extract the simple "aha!" moments behind the dense equations.
*   **From Scratch & Experiments:** We won't just talk theory. We’ll build from scratch, revisit landmark papers, and experiment with code to see these concepts in action.

I believe that a deep understanding combined with hands-on experimentation is the key to mastering AI. I'm excited to share my research, my failures, and my breakthroughs with you.
